## 🔧 Important: Complete Domain Setup

### Your domain is ready in Vercel, but you need to update your DNS

**⚠️ NEXT STEP - Update Your Domain Registrar**

Your domain `cloudfordschool.com` is currently using old nameservers:
- dns1.registrar-servers.com
- dns2.registrar-servers.com

To make your site live on cloudfordschool.com, update your domain registrar's nameservers to **Vercel's nameservers**:

**Change your nameservers to:**
```
ns1.vercel.sh
ns2.vercel.sh
ns3.vercel.sh
```

### How to Update Nameservers:
1. Go to your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google Domains, etc.)
2. Find "Nameservers" or "DNS Settings"
3. Replace existing nameservers with Vercel's nameservers above
4. Save the changes
5. Wait 24-48 hours for DNS propagation

### ✅ Once DNS Propagates:
Your site will be fully live at **cloudfordschool.com**
